  <div class="col-md-6 px-0">
    <h1 class="display-4 font-italic">We believe</h1>
    <p class="lead my-3">CivicDataLab works with the goal to use data, tech, design and social science to strengthen the course of civic engagements in India. We work to harness the potential of open-source movement to enable citizens to engage better with public reforms. We aim to grow data and tech literacy of governments, nonprofits, think-tanks, media houses, universities etc to enable data-driven decision making at scale.</p>
