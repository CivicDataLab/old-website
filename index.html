About Us

CivicDataLab works with goal to use data, tech, design and social science to the course of civic engagements in India. We work to harness potential of open-source movement to enable citizens engage better with public reforms. We aim to grow data and tech literacy of governments, nonprofits, think-tanks, media houses, universities etc to enable data driven decision making at scale.
